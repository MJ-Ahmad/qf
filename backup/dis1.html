Signature Folding

Printed sheets are folded into signatures and gathered in sequence. Skilled technicians ensure page order and register are perfect before binding.

Inspection and Continuity Check

Each complete Mushaf is inspected page by page for continuity, print defects, and pagination errors. Quality assurance teams verify that no pages are missing or out of sequence.

Cover Design and Printing

Covers are printed with durable inks and materials, combining traditional ornamentation with modern durability standards.

Casing In and Binding

Signatures are sewn or glued, then cased into printed covers. Binding techniques preserve page order and allow the Mushaf to open flat while maintaining structural integrity.

Adhesive Curing & Final Drying

After casing and gluing, Mushafs enter a controlled drying phase to ensure adhesives cure properly and bindings remain secure over time.

Final Binding Inspection & Storage

Completed Mushafs undergo a final binding inspection. Approved copies are organized and stored in climate-controlled conditions to preserve quality until distribution.

Packing & Cartoning

Mushafs are carefully packed into cartons with protective materials. Cartoning follows distribution plans to ensure safe transport to local and international destinations.

Distribution & Community Delivery

Final distribution places Mushafs into the hands of students, mosques, and community centers. We coordinate with local partners to ensure respectful and effective delivery.
