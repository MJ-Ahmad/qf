Our Mission — Preserving, Publishing, and Distributing the Holy Quran

Quraner Fariwala is Bangladesh’s leading institution for Quranic research, premium printing, and global distribution. We combine classical scholarship with modern production and distribution systems to ensure accurate, accessible, and memorization‑friendly Qurans reach learners, Hifz students, and vulnerable communities worldwide.

Advanced Quranic Research

Conduct thematic tafsir, linguistic analysis of Quranic Arabic, and comparative methodologies — producing peer‑reviewed studies, annotated editions, and teacher resources that strengthen understanding and pedagogy.

Premium, Memorization‑Optimized Printing

Produce high‑quality, durable Qurans with clear typography, tajweed markers, and memorization aids. Our editions are optimized for learners and institutions, with accessible formats and rigorous quality control.

Global Distribution & Community Programs

Deliver Qurans to schools, orphanages, and Hifz centers through targeted partnerships and subsidized programs. We prioritize underserved regions and provide teacher training and community support.
