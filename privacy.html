1. Information We Collect

Personal Information: Name, email, contact details, profile information for Quraner Fariwala initiatives, payment details (processed securely), communication preferences, and feedback.

Usage Information: Pages visited, features used, device details, browser type, IP address, general location, and interactions with competitions and community features.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide and improve Quraner Fariwala’s services
  • Facilitate participation in Qur’an competitions and initiatives
  • Send important updates and communications
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Prevent fraud and ensure platform security
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Information may be shared only in these limited circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal requirements
  • With trusted service providers who assist our operations
  • In case of business transfer or merger
  • To protect our rights and prevent fraud

4. Data Security

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication systems
  • Secure data centers and backup systems
  • Employee training on data protection

5. Your Rights

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Object to certain data processing

6. Cookies & Tracking

Quraner Fariwala uses cookies and similar technologies to improve user experience. Cookies help remember preferences, track usage, and personalize services. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Children under 13 may only participate with parental or guardian consent. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children without proper authorization.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date. Significant changes will be communicated directly to registered users.
