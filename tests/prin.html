Step-by-Step Production

Every Qur'an goes through a meticulous 6-step process to ensure exceptional quality and care.

1
Pre-Press & Design

Pre-Press & Design

Our expert typesetters work with certified Islamic scholars to ensure every letter, diacritical mark, and spacing adheres to traditional Qur'anic calligraphy standards. The layout is optimized for memorization with clear verse markers and spacious design.

  • Certified Arabic typography specialists
  • Multi-stage scholarly review process
  • Memorization-optimized page layout
  • 21cm x 14cm format with 668 pages
2
Offset Printing

Offset Printing

Using state-of-the-art 4-color offset printing technology, each page is rendered with exceptional clarity on 61 GSM archival-grade paper. This ensures durability for years of daily use while maintaining comfortable page weight.

  • 4-color offset printing technology
  • 61 GSM archival-grade paper
  • Crystal-clear text reproduction
  • Consistent ink density throughout
3
Binding & Assembly

Binding & Assembly

Skilled craftsmen carefully bind each volume using durable binding techniques that allow pages to lay flat for comfortable reading. The protective cover is designed to withstand daily handling while maintaining an elegant appearance.

  • Professional thread binding
  • Lay-flat spine design
  • Durable hardcover protection
  • Gold foil stamping on cover
4
Quality Control

Quality Control

Every single copy undergoes rigorous quality inspection. Our QC team checks for print clarity, binding integrity, page alignment, and overall finish. Only volumes meeting our exacting standards proceed to packaging.

  • 100% inspection rate
  • Print clarity verification
  • Binding integrity testing
  • Page alignment checks
5
Packaging

Packaging

Each Qur'an is carefully placed in a custom slipcase for protection during transport. Volumes are then boxed in batches of 50 for efficient logistics while ensuring each copy arrives in pristine condition.

  • Individual protective slipcases
  • Batch packaging (50 copies/box)
  • Moisture-resistant materials
  • Labeled for tracking
6
Distribution

Distribution

Our distribution network ensures equitable delivery to rural madrasas, urban schools, and community centers across Bangladesh. Priority is given to orphaned students and underserved Hifz institutions.

  • Priority to orphan institutions
  • Nationwide distribution network
  • Signed receipt documentation
  • Photo verification at delivery
