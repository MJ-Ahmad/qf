Our Production Process

From careful typesetting to final delivery, every step in our process is designed to ensure the highest quality Qur'ans reach those who need them most.

Step-by-Step Production

Every Qur'an goes through a meticulous 11-step process to ensure exceptional quality and care.

1

Pre-Press & Design

Sheets are imposed into forms (e.g., 23/36 format) and printed with precise registration. This stage sets the foundation for accurate pagination and folding.

  • Certified Arabic typography specialists
  • Multi-stage scholarly review process
  • Memorization-optimized page layout
2

Offset Printing

Using state-of-the-art 4-color offset printing technology, each page is rendered with exceptional clarity on 61 GSM archival-grade paper.

  • 4-color offset printing technology
  • 61 GSM archival-grade paper
  • Crystal-clear text reproduction
  • Consistent ink density throughout
