Our Mission — Preserve, Publish, Distribute

Quraner Fariwala is Bangladesh’s leading institution for Quranic research, premium printing, and global distribution. We combine classical scholarship with modern production and distribution systems to ensure accurate, accessible, and memorization‑friendly Qurans reach learners, Hifz students, and vulnerable communities worldwide.

Advanced Quranic Research

Produce peer‑reviewed tafsir, linguistic studies, and annotated editions that strengthen teaching and deepen understanding. We publish teacher guides, scholarly articles, and accessible resources for institutions.

Premium, Memorization‑Optimized Printing

High‑quality Qurans with clear typography, tajweed markers, and memorization aids. Durable materials and accessible formats ensure long‑lasting, learner‑friendly editions for schools and homes.

Global Distribution & Community Programs

Deliver Qurans to schools, orphanages, and Hifz centers through partnerships and subsidized programs. We prioritize underserved regions and support teacher training and community engagement.
