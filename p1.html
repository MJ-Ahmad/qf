Mushaf Printing and Distribution

QuranerFariwala combines traditional scholarship, modern printing standards, and rigorous quality control to produce authentic, beautifully printed Mushafs and distribute them responsibly to communities worldwide. Below is a clear, step-by-step presentation of our printing workflow, accompanied by photographic documentation of each stage.

Printing Plate and Imposition

The process begins with layout and imposition: Quran pages are arranged into signatures (e.g., 23/36 format) and prepared for press. This image shows a printed sheet laid out in form ready for folding and trimming.

Signature Folding

Printed sheets are folded into signatures with precise alignment. Skilled technicians fold each sheet to ensure page order and register are perfect before binding.

Inspection and Page Continuity Check

After folding, each complete Mushaf is inspected page by page for continuity, print defects, and pagination errors. Quality assurance teams verify that no pages are missing or out of sequence.

Cover Design and Printing

Covers are designed with durable materials and printed with attention to typography and ornamentation. This stage ensures the Mushaf is both beautiful and long-lasting.

Casing In and Binding

Each folded signature is bound and cased into its cover. Binding techniques preserve page order and allow the Mushaf to open flat while maintaining structural integrity.

Adhesive Curing and Final Drying

After casing and gluing, Mushafs enter a controlled drying phase to ensure adhesives cure properly and bindings remain secure over time.

Final Binding Inspection and Storage

Completed Mushafs undergo a final binding inspection. Approved copies are organized and stored in climate-controlled conditions to preserve quality until distribution.

Packing and Cartoning

Mushafs are carefully packed into cartons with protective materials. Cartoning follows distribution plans to ensure safe transport to local and international destinations.

Distribution and Community Delivery

Final distribution places Mushafs into the hands of students, mosques, and community centers. We coordinate with local partners to ensure respectful and effective delivery.
